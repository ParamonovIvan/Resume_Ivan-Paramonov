BREWING

I became acquainted with craft beer in 2015, after which my attitude towards this drink changed radically. I spent two years tasting different styles, trying more than 400 different samples. The love for beer gave rise to the desire to brew beer myself. So I became a home brewer and founded my own brewery, Paranoid Brewery (since 2017). The volume of home equipment is 40 liters (output). To date, 350 beer brews have been made at home (12 tons of finished product). Periodically brewed beer at microbreweries, under contract or in collaborations.

I love brewing different styles of beer, changing recipes, using different ingredients. Here are some of the varieties:

COLLABORATIONS

Communication and getting to know other brewers (both home brewers and small brewers) often leads to collaborations (joint brews). Collaborations allow you to exchange ideas and experiment. For example, beer with the addition of grapefruit and beets was brewed several times together with the Kirov brewery “The Ded and Paddle”, in Izhevsk with the brewery “JFF” - a stout with the addition of a huge amount of cocoa beans, and in Perm with the brewery “Forbidden Beer” – dubbel with dates and raisins.

The Dad and Paddle

Double Impact (Grapefruit DIPA)

Kirov, 200 l, 25.01.2020
Kirov, 1000 l, 20.11.2021

Ivan Dudakov

Ambassador, Reload Brewery

My name is Ivan, and I have a long-standing and strong friendship with beer. This is a hobby, a job, and a lifestyle. I met my namesake from Paranoid brewery quite by accident - after seeing a story with his beer from a mutual friend. We are from different cities, I am from Kemerovo, and Ivan is from Izhevsk. But this did not stop me from ordering all the varieties available at that time for testing. A year later, and a couple more orders from Ivan, we had the chance to meet in person. Every summer I go on a motorcycle trip to new cities and towns. That year Izhevsk was also on the route. And of course, I did not expect that Ivan would invite me to visit him, to the holy of holies - to his brewery. And although it is located in an apartment, its scale is amazing. Cooking takes place in the kitchen, another brewed variety is being cooled in the bathroom, and in the living room you can taste the latest new products among picturesque barrels and bottles of all kinds. In a word - Paranoid brewery is craft in the full sense of the word.

MUSIC

Another one of my favorite activities is playing the saxophone. It started at the age of 19 when I wanted to play wind instruments. So I studied with a teacher for one year and learned to play the trumpet, and then I mastered the saxophone on my own. I play in the jazz orchestra "Big Band of Izhevsk State Technical University" and in the duet "... and Ivan on sax". We perform with the orchestra at jazz festivals and city celebrations, solo and in duets at celebrations (in bars, cafes and restaurants).
